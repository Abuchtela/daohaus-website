The DAOhaus ecosystem is mainly made up of: 

- **UberHaus**: The DAO of DAOs that governs the DAOhaus platform and HAUS token 
- **Warcamp DAO**: The development team that builds and maintain the DAOhaus platform 
- **Warcamp Circles**: Teams within Warcamp that work on respective workstreams

![UberHaus, Ecosystems and Circles](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/827263928882429952/870704417551302687/DAOhaus_Economy_01.png)

## Warcamp Circles

### Alchemists

Alchemists are the token economists. They are economists that methodically oversee the HAUS token, its governance and guide DAOs on their own tokenomics and microeconomics.

### Magesmiths

Magesmiths are builders. They are crafters of code and user experience who ensure our platform provides a seamless experience for our members.

### Paladins

Paladins are operators. They are defenders of the realm who gather resources for our budget, coordinate schedules and lead workshops for the community. They ensure we can focus on creating value for our members.

### Rangers

Rangers are communicators. They are wordsmiths that craft our wilds ideas into consumable content for all and cultivate the HausParty vibes.

## UberHAUS

### UberPaladins

UberPaladins are coordinators. They are the task masters that schedule UberHaus meetings and ensure UberHaus is meeting its goal as the DAO of DAOs governing the DAOhaus platform.
